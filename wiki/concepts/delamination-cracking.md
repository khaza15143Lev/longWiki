# Delamination Cracking

Horizontal separation within a concrete slab at or near dowel depth, distinct from vertical longitudinal cracking.

## Mechanism

Delamination occurs at the plane of dowel placement due to stress concentrations from vertical dowel translation, consolidation issues around dowels, or freeze-thaw action at the dowel-concrete interface.

## Key Findings

- [[rao-abdualla-sufian-2024-dowel-tie-bar-cracking]] identified delamination at dowel depth as distinct failure mode from joint failure; developed ACPA trigger/variant framework for diagnosis
- [[seo-kim-2013-dowel-longitudinal-cracking]] found vertical dowel translation creates localized transverse stress that can initiate delamination
