# Dowel Bar Misalignment

Dowel bars placed outside specified tolerances for position, orientation, or depth, reducing load transfer and inducing stress concentrations.

## Mechanism

Misaligned dowels restrict slab movement, create stress concentrations at the dowel-concrete interface, and reduce load transfer efficiency. Vertical misalignment causes dowels to bind under thermal expansion, leading to spalling and cracking.

## Key Findings

- [[tayabji-trr-1062]] documented dowel placement tolerances and consequences of misalignment for concrete pavements
- [[seo-kim-2013-dowel-longitudinal-cracking]] found vertical dowel translation (from misalignment) causes localized transverse stress initiating longitudinal cracking
- [[rao-abdualla-sufian-2024-dowel-tie-bar-cracking]] identified misaligned dowels as a trigger for delamination at dowel depth
