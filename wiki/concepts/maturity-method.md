# Maturity Method

Non-destructive technique for estimating concrete strength at early ages based on time-temperature history during curing.

## Mechanism

The maturity method correlates concrete strength development with the product of time and temperature (maturity index). It allows real-time strength estimation without breaking test cylinders, enabling optimized saw-cutting timing.

## Key Findings

- [[lim-tayabji-2005-early-age-longitudinal-cracking]] referenced maturity method for determining optimal saw-cut timing to prevent early-age cracking
- [[mateos-harvey-2021-early-age-premature-cracking]] discussed maturity method as a construction quality control tool
