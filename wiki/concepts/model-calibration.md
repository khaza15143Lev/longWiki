# Model Calibration

Process of adjusting mechanistic-empirical pavement design model parameters to match local materials, climate, and performance data.

## Mechanism

Calibration involves comparing predicted distress levels (cracking, faulting, IRI) with observed field performance from test sections or in-service pavements. Transfer functions are adjusted to minimize prediction error.

## Key Findings

- [[saboori-et-al-2021-pavement-me-calibration]] calibrated AASHTOWare Pavement ME for California conditions; found widened shoulders perform poorly in SP-2 sections and Pavement ME cannot predict longitudinal cracking for JPCP
- [[mateos-harvey-2021-early-age-premature-cracking]] identified gap in Pavement ME's ability to model early-age construction-related distresses without local calibration
