# PCA Fatigue Equation

Empirical fatigue equation developed by the Portland Cement Association relating concrete stress ratio to allowable number of load repetitions before fatigue failure.

## Equation

The PCA fatigue equation relates the ratio of applied flexural stress to concrete modulus of rupture (MR) to the number of load repetitions to failure. It is the basis for traditional fatigue-based pavement design.

## Limitations

- Does not account for environmental loading (curling, warping, shrinkage)
- Assumes bottom-up fatigue cracking; does not predict top-down longitudinal cracking
- Used as baseline for comparison in JSLAB and other FEA analyses

## Key Findings

- [[ozbeki-et-al-trr-1043]] referenced PCA fatigue equation in evaluation methodology for jointed concrete pavements; noted limitations for longitudinal cracking prediction
