# Shoulder Design

Configuration and material selection for pavement shoulders, influencing edge support and longitudinal cracking potential.

## Types

- **Tied PCC shoulder**: Structurally connected to main slab via tie bars; provides continuous support and load transfer
- **HMA shoulder**: Asphalt shoulder not tied to slab; provides minimal load transfer (LTE ~10%)
- **Granular shoulder**: Unbound aggregate; provides minimal support and no load transfer

## Key Findings

- [[kaya-et-al-2019-widened-jpcp-longitudinal-cracking]] found tied PCC shoulders outperform HMA and granular shoulders; widened or regular slabs with HMA shoulders demonstrate similar behavior to no shoulder
- [[yang-et-al-2020-widened-concrete-pavements]] recommended tied PCC shoulders for widened JPCP with high truck traffic; granular shoulders should be avoided
