# Stabilized Base

Subbase layer constructed with bound materials (HMA, lean concrete, or cement-treated aggregate) to provide uniform support and resist erosion.

## Mechanism

Stabilized bases resist pumping and erosion better than granular bases, but can fail catastrophically when drainage is inadequate. HMA and PCC stabilized bases have different erosion characteristics.

## Key Findings

- [[desantis-roesler-2022-stabilized-support-layers]] found HMA/PCC subbase erosion directly correlates with longitudinal cracking in poor drainage areas
- [[yang-et-al-2020-widened-concrete-pavements]] noted stabilized bases perform better than granular bases but require adequate drainage to prevent catastrophic failure
