# Colorado Department of Transportation (CDOT)

Colorado's state transportation agency, responsible for highway design, construction, and maintenance. CDOT's Research Branch conducts studies on pavement performance and distress mechanisms.

## Role in Wiki Sources

- Commissioned and authored the 2003 study on premature PCCP longitudinal cracking in Region 1, resulting in two new specifications for saw-cut depth monitoring and vibrator frequency devices ([[ardani-et-al-2003-colorado]]).
- Adopted 14-ft slab design in 1996 based on [[ltpp-sps-2]] research and work by [[michael-darter]].
