# Construction Technology Laboratory (CTL)

A division of the Portland Cement Association (PCA). Conducts research, testing, and analysis on concrete pavement materials and structures.

## Role in Wiki Sources

- Developed the JSLAB finite element program for analyzing jointed concrete pavements with load transfer devices ([[ozbeki-et-al-trr-1043]])
- Performed petrographic analysis of cores from vibrator trails in the CDOT longitudinal cracking study ([[ardani-et-al-2003-colorado]])
