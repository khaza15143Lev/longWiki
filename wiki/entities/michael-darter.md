# Michael Darter

Pavement engineering researcher associated with ERES (consulting/research firm). Conducted supplemental studies supporting the 14-ft wide slab design for rural highways.

## Role in Wiki Sources

- Research cited by CDOT as basis for adopting 14-ft slab design in 1996 ([[ardani-et-al-2003-colorado]], p. 11).
- Work connected to [[ltpp-sps-2]] findings on wider slab performance.
