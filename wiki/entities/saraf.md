# Saraf, Chhote L.

Pavement engineering researcher. Co-developed the probabilistic model for predicting longitudinal crack confinement in saw-cut joints with B. Frank McCullough.

## Role in Wiki Sources

- Developed statistical model using variability in concrete tensile strength and pavement thickness to estimate saw-cut depth needed for crack control ([[ozbeki-et-al-trr-1043]])
- Verified model against two Texas highway projects (TX-288 near Houston)
