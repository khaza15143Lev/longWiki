# Tayabji, Shiraz D.

Pavement engineering researcher at Construction Technology Laboratories (PCA). Co-developed the JSLAB finite element program with B.E. Colley.

## Role in Wiki Sources

- Co-developed [[jslab]] finite element program for analyzing jointed concrete pavements ([[ozbeki-et-al-trr-1043]])
- Conducted comprehensive investigation into dowel placement tolerances through CTL under FHWA sponsorship ([[tayabji-trr-1062]])
- Published field evaluation methods for dowel misalignment in Concrete International (Jan. 1986)
